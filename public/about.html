FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Got Questions?

Find answers to common questions about our DSA courses and platform.

What is the duration of the DSA course?

Our comprehensive DSA course is designed as a self-paced program spanning 4 phases. Most students complete the entire curriculum in 3-6 months, depending on their prior experience and time commitment. You get lifetime access, so you can learn at your own pace without any time pressure.

Do I need prior programming experience?

Basic programming knowledge in at least one language (Python, Java, or C++) is recommended. You should be comfortable with fundamental concepts like variables, loops, and functions. Our Phase 01 starts with foundations, making it accessible for beginners while still valuable for experienced developers.

What programming languages are supported?

All solutions and code examples are provided in three popular languages: Python, Java, and C++. You can switch between languages based on your preference or interview requirements. The algorithmic concepts remain the same across all languages.

How does the progress tracking work?

Each student has a personalized dashboard that tracks your progress across all topics and questions. When you mark a problem as solved, it's saved to your account. You can see completion percentages, difficulty breakdowns, and your overall progress through the curriculum at any time.

Are the problems from real interviews?

Yes! Our curriculum includes 500+ curated problems sourced from actual technical interviews at FAANG companies (Facebook/Meta, Amazon, Apple, Netflix, Google) and other top tech companies. Each problem is linked to LeetCode for practice and verification.

What makes AIONYX different from other platforms?

AIONYX focuses on pattern-based learning rather than random problem-solving. Our structured 4-phase curriculum ensures you understand the fundamental architecture of algorithms. We provide visual explanations, multiple language support, personalized progress tracking, and a beautiful, modern learning interface—all for a one-time payment with lifetime access.

Is there any support or mentorship available?

Yes! You get access to our exclusive Discord community with 10,000+ engineers, where you can ask questions, participate in code reviews, and join system design discussions. Our trainers are active in the community and provide guidance on complex topics.

What is the pricing model?

AIONYX offers a one-time payment of $50 for lifetime access to the entire platform. No monthly subscriptions, no hidden fees. You get access to all 500+ problems, video walkthroughs, solutions in multiple languages, and all future updates—forever.
